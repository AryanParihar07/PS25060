Clean & Green: Waste Management Solutions

Solutions Digital App Video

Problem Statement Overview

In India, a significant amount of the 1.7 lakh tonnes of daily municipal solid waste remains untreated, posing severe environmental risks. A substantial gap exists in effective waste management, with a large portion either dumped in landfills or remaining unaccounted for. This project proposes a comprehensive, citizen-centric approach to bridge this gap through training, community participation, and a dedicated digital system.

Key Proposed Solutions

1. Mandatory Citizen Training

Compulsory waste management training for every citizen to ensure source segregation, home composting, and reuse of materials.

  • Physical training on waste types and segregation.
  • Distribution of 3 dustbins for segregated waste.
  • Compost making kits for every household.
  • App-based monitoring system.

2. Phase-wise Training for Workers

Structured training to all waste workers, focusing on safety, proper handling, and efficient collection methods to improve the entire waste management chain.

3. "Green Champions" Committee

Formation of decentralized area committees to monitor waste management at every step, from generation to disposal.

  • Monitoring of source segregation.
  • Ensuring efficient waste collection & transportation.
  • Supervision of waste treatment and disposal.

4. Incentive Based System

Rewarding bulk waste generators and other buildings for implementing effective source segregation, encouraging proactive participation.

5. "If you see waste, send photo"

A mandatory community-driven movement in every Urban Local Body (ULB) to report illegal dumping sites, inspired by Yadgir city's model.

6. Community Participation

Mandating one day of public cleaning for all working staff, starting with the government sector, to instill a sense of shared responsibility.

7. Penalization System

Imposing fines and stopping waste collection for buildings that fail to segregate waste at the source, serving as a strict deterrent.

8. Waste Management Facilities

Ensuring the availability of essential facilities like Biomethanization plants, W-to-E plants, and multiple recycling centers in every ULB.

9. Complete Digital App-based System

A centralized digital platform to manage all aspects of waste management, from training to monitoring and tracking.

Training & Shopping

Training modules for citizens and an online store for waste utilities.

Tracking & Location

Real-time tracking of collection vehicles and a map of facilities and scrap shops.

Reporting

Option to upload geo-tagged photos of dumping sites for immediate action.
